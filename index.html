ECO-TIPS !!!

Here are small changes you can make every day to help protect the environment!!

  1. Reduce Plastic
  2. Save Water
  3. Reuse items

1. Reduce Plastic Use

Bring your own shopping bags, avoid plastic straws, and choose products with less packaging. Every little bit helps reduce ocean pollution.


2. Save Water Daily

Turn off taps when brushing, fix leaks, and use water-efficient devices. Water is precious — let’s not waste it.


3. Reuse What You Can

Use glass jars, refillable bottles, and repurpose containers. Reusing saves money and keeps waste out of landfills.
